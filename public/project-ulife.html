UTP's ULife Mobile App

ULife, the exclusive mobile app for Universiti Teknologi PETRONAS, is your one-stop shop for a seamless university experience. Designed for both staff and students, ULife fosters a connected community by offering a variety of features:

• Centralized Platform: Manage your academic life, access university resources, and stay updated on campus news - all within a single, user-friendly app.

• Enhanced Engagement: ULife acts as a bridge between students, staff, and management, facilitating communication and collaboration.

• AI-Powered Helpline: Get instant support with ULife's innovative customer service helpline powered by Gemini, a powerful AI technology. Gemini understands your questions and provides prompt and accurate answers.

• Built with Speed and Efficiency: Developed using Dart and Flutter, ULife delivers a smooth and responsive experience on all mobile devices.
